NS Atlanta North District

The Atlanta North District is the ex-Southern mainline from Atlanta, GA to Chattanooga, TN. It sees ~45 trains a day between Atlanta and Austell, and ~25 between Austell and Chattanooga.

Hardman Drive

NS Atlanta North Distict, Milepost 108.0

‹ Next Coordinates Previous ›

At the summit of Braswell Mountain, Hardman Drive is one of those out of the way spots that you'd need someone to show you to find. It has a fantastic southbound shot with an S curve and trains often pull hard up the grade.
